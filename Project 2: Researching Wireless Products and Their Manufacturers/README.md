# Key Wireless Products and Manufacturers

This repository contains research on key wireless products, identifying their manufacturers, official documentation, and primary functions within the industry.

---

### 1. LoRaWAN Gateway
* **Manufacturer:** Kerlink
* **Link:** [Kerlink Wirnet iStation Official Page](https://www.kerlink.com/product/wirnet-istation/)
* **Description:** A LoRaWAN gateway acts as a bridge between low-power end-devices and a network server, allowing for long-range data transmission in IoT networks. Kerlink's gateways are built for industrial-grade reliability and feature high interference resistance for dense outdoor deployments.

### 2. Zigbee Module
* **Manufacturer:** Digi International
* **Link:** [Digi XBee3 Zigbee 3.0 Official Page](https://www.digi.com/products/embedded-systems/digi-xbee/rf-modules/2-4-ghz-rf-modules/xbee3-zigbee-3)
* **Description:** Zigbee modules provide short-range, low-power mesh networking capabilities suitable for home automation and industrial sensor monitoring. The XBee3 series is notable for its compact form factor and the ability to switch between multiple protocols using the same hardware.

### 3. Wi-Fi 6 Chipset
* **Manufacturer:** Qualcomm
* **Link:** [Qualcomm FastConnect 6900 Official Page](https://www.qualcomm.com/wi-fi/products/fastconnect/fastconnect-6900)
* **Description:** This chipset manages high-speed wireless connectivity in mobile devices, supporting the 802.11ax standard for improved efficiency. It features 4-stream Dual Band Simultaneous (DBS) technology to provide multi-gigabit speeds and VR-class low latency.

### 4. NB-IoT Module
* **Manufacturer:** Quectel
* **Link:** [Quectel BC660K-GL Official Page](https://www.quectel.com/blog/document/lpwa-bc660k-gl-nb2/)
* **Description:** NB-IoT modules enable cellular connectivity for massive IoT deployments that require deep indoor penetration and long battery life. The BC660K-GL is an ultra-compact module that supports multiple frequency bands with extremely low power consumption.

### 5. Sigfox Device
* **Manufacturer:** UnaBiz (Sigfox)
* **Link:** [Sens'it Discovery Official Page](https://partners.sigfox.com/products/sensit-discovery)
* **Description:** Sigfox devices utilize a global Ultra-Narrowband network to transmit small data bursts over very long distances with minimal energy. The Sens'it is a multi-sensor device featuring a thermometer, hygrometer, and accelerometer for environmental monitoring without local pairing.

### 6. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon
* **Manufacturer:** Kontakt.io
* **Link:** [Kontakt.io Smart Beacon 2 Official Page](https://kontakt.io/blog/smart_beacon_two/)
* **Description:** BLE beacons are small transmitters that broadcast signals to nearby smart devices for indoor positioning and asset tracking. The Smart Beacon 2 features a dual-battery design for a 4-year lifespan and broadcasts both iBeacon and Eddystone formats simultaneously.

### 7. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Transceiver
* **Manufacturer:** Qorvo
* **Link:** [Qorvo DW3110 Official Page](https://www.qorvo.com/products/p/DW3110)
* **Description:** UWB transceivers provide highly accurate spatial awareness and micro-location data, often precise to within 10 centimeters. The DW3000 series is designed for low-power operation and is optimized for automotive and mobile "find-my" applications.

### 8. Wi-Fi 6E Access Point

* **Link:** [Aruba 630 Series Campus AP Official Page](https://www.arubanetworks.com/products/wireless/access-points/indoor-access-points/630-series/)

### 9. 5G IoT Module
* **Manufacturer:** Telit Cinterion
* **Link:** [Telit FN990A Series Official Page](https://www.telit.com/devices/fn990axx/)
* **Description:** 5G IoT modules provide high-bandwidth, low-latency connectivity for demanding applications like industrial robotics and 4K/8K video surveillance. The FN990A series is an industrial-grade M.2 data card supporting 3GPP Release 16 for global 5G Sub-6 deployments.
